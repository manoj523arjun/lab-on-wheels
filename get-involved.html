Corporate Support

Our initiative is focused on a vision to make healthcare accessible to our fellow citizens in remote areas of our country. We aim to provide many mobile lab units across the country and enable the government to deploy them in areas where there is a genuine need. We partner with Corporate customers in their CSR activities to make a difference to local communities in the area of Health and Hygiene. We believe, corporate contributions go a long way in impacting lives and livelihoods.

  • Impacting Lives

    Your funds act as a catalyst and can accelerate the reach of this initiative.

  • Securing Livelihoods

    Your contributions will not just save lives but will save livelihoods of people.

  • Creating Jobs

    This initiative needs local participation and every mobile unit will create local jobs under the Skill India Program.
