About Us

Our founders were moved by the lack of basic affordable diagnostic facilities in remote areas of India. They have seen families suffer mentally & monetarily because of illnesses and they have made it their life mission to help such communities that need affordable early diagnosis in collaboration with the communities. This commitment has grown over years and evolved into - Saicorp Health Technologies.

Saicorp Health Technologies has developed “Lab built on Wheels" to serve the rural population with a laboratory on a trailer, which is a first of its kind in this country. These mobile lab units, developed by Saicorp Industries (India's leading trailer manufacturer) is made as a trailer with state-of-the-art equipment with high safety standards and are rugged enough to provide years of service. This is a unit which can be tagged to a tractor or a jeep and moved to remote areas, catering to all the health initiatives of the government. These can be rapidly deployed during any endemic/epidemic or pandemic situation. It serves as a single moving laboratory for the district to deliver diagnostic services and stem the spread of communicable diseases.

Why Lab on Wheels

Indian healthcare system is undergoing changes in the context of ever-increasing chronic disease burdens and healthcare costs. Many illnesses disproportionately affect certain populations, due to disparities in healthcare access and social determinants of health.

  • Communicable diseases affect certain populations at large and disparities are significant in rural population.
  • 85% - 90% of the infected population is not diagnosed in its disease infancy.
  • Over 65% of the rural population in India have far lesser access to health care facilities than those living in the cities.
  • These disparities represent a key area to target in order to better our nation's overall health and decrease healthcare expenditures.

Lab Built on Wheels serves to fulfil needs of the needy in Villages, Taluks and Districts to serve mainly who are not able to travel to distant places for the national healthcare programs as announced time to time by MoHFW, Government of Karnataka or Union Government.

This mobile laboratory is a microbiology lab used to detect disease-causing bacteria, fungi or virus in public health initiatives or due to endemic, epidemic or in pandemic situations.
